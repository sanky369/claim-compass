## Product Direction

The hackathon build is the focused wedge: denial decoding, next-best-action triage, corrected-claim or appeal guidance, repeat-denial rule creation, and MongoDB-backed traceability. The full product can expand from this backbone into credentialing health, eligibility checks, payer follow-up, and cash-flow risk.
